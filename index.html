Our mission

Our mission is to provide customers with one of a kind experience when ordering foods in a accesible location and offers affordable but delicious snacks to customers. Uniqueness makes everything stand out and as for the Shanica Snack house, good quality of nutritious food depends as well to the ambiance and the environment of the customers. The enterprise wants to serve a good nutritious food, at a good price and a good place.

Our Vision

Our vision is to give nutritious food snack while enjoying the ambiance of the store and also to see the customer satisfaction and appeals to every product the store produce and to be a competitive market in the food line.

About us

The Shanica snack house is a small food business that will be launched on the year 2020. The business was owned by 3 individuals and decided to be Partners. Having a contribution of 5000 pesos individually to settle their source of capital amounting to 10000 pesos. First we open a burgers only. When the capital is enough we decide to open a new menu which is rice meals, milk shake, nachos and other fingerFoods. Since we are home made people love our burgers for sure.
The Establishment will be located at Hedriana St., Purok 1, Pavia, Iloilo and the risk is relatively neutral as the location has strengths in terms of attracting customers.
